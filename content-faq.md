## FAQ Items

### What cigar brands does CNX Cigars carry?
CNX Cigars carries a curated selection of the world's finest brands, including Cuban marques such as Cohiba, Montecristo, Partagas, Romeo y Julieta, and Hoyo de Monterrey, alongside premium New World brands including Padron, Arturo Fuente, Davidoff, Oliva, My Father, Rocky Patel, and Ashton.

The selection is updated regularly based on availability and quality. Browse the collection or contact us directly to ask about a specific vitola.

### What is the age policy?
CNX Cigars is an adults-only establishment. Under Thai law, you must be 20 years of age or older to purchase or consume tobacco products. Valid ID may be requested. This policy is non-negotiable and applies to all visitors.

### Do you offer delivery or online ordering?
CNX Cigars is a lounge-first experience, not an e-commerce operation. We do not offer online ordering. For regular clients and B2B partners, supply arrangements can be discussed directly. Contact us to enquire.

### How do I book a private event or tasting?
Private tastings and events can be arranged by contacting us via the contact form, WhatsApp, or phone. We accommodate groups from 4 to approximately 30 guests depending on the format.

We recommend at least one week's notice for small groups, and two to four weeks for larger events or bespoke programmes.

### Is it legal to smoke cigars in Thailand?
Yes. Smoking cigars is legal in Thailand in designated smoking areas. CNX Cigars operates as a licensed tobacco retail and lounge venue in full compliance with Thai tobacco law. Smoking is permitted within our lounge premises.

Thai law prohibits the sale of tobacco to persons under 20 years of age and restricts tobacco advertising   which is why you will not find us running promotions in the traditional sense.

### What do cigars cost at CNX Cigars?
Price ranges vary by brand and vitola   from accessible entry-level cigars starting around 200 THB to rare and premium Cuban marques at 1,500 THB and above. Our aim is to have something for every level of interest and budget.

Browse the collection for an indication of price ranges, or ask us directly for current pricing.

### Do I need to know about cigars to visit?
Absolutely not. CNX Cigars is built on the idea of a private club with an open door. Whether this is your first cigar or your thousandth, you will be looked after   without gatekeeping, without pressure, and without theatre.

Amir and the team are happy to guide newcomers from their very first question to their first light.

### Can you help set up a cigar programme for my hotel or restaurant?
Yes. Amir offers B2B consulting for hotels, restaurants, and hospitality venues across Northern Thailand. This covers programme design, stock curation, humidification infrastructure, and staff training.

Contact us to start a conversation, or read more about the service.

### Do I need to book in advance?
Walk-ins are welcome throughout opening hours   no booking is required for individual visits. For private tastings, group visits, or events, we ask for advance notice. A quick WhatsApp message is the easiest way to let us know you are coming.
