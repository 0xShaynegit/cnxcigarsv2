The founder

Amir Nadimi

"There is a fundamental difference between a purveyor and an authority. To be a purveyor is to manage inventory. To be an authority is to curate an experience, preserve a culture, and pass down the nuanced art of the leaf."

Amir Nadimi founded CNX Cigars on thirty years with the leaf, and on a single, clear idea: that the best cigar is the one shared. He built this lounge as a community, not a shop. A confluence point for expatriates, travellers, and local connoisseurs who understand that slowing down to smoke a fine cigar is an act of mindfulness.

He speaks the language of draw, construction, and flavour evolution with the precision of a craftsman. Whether you are exploring your first cigar or chasing a rare vitola, the knowledge on offer here is real and freely shared.

CNX Cigars carries a curated selection including Cuban marques such as Cohiba, Montecristo, Partagas, Romeo y Julieta, and Hoyo de Monterrey, alongside premium New World brands including Padron, Arturo Fuente, Davidoff, Oliva, My Father, Rocky Patel, and Ashton. The selection is updated regularly.

CNX Cigars is an adults-only establishment. Under Thai law, you must be 20 years of age or older to purchase or consume tobacco products. Valid ID may be requested. This policy is non-negotiable.

CNX Cigars is a lounge-first experience, not an e-commerce operation. We do not offer online ordering. For regular clients and B2B partners, supply arrangements can be discussed directly.

Private tastings and events can be arranged via the contact form, WhatsApp, or phone. We accommodate groups from 4 to approximately 30 guests. At least one week's notice for small groups, two to four weeks for larger events.

Yes. Smoking cigars is legal in Thailand in designated smoking areas. CNX Cigars operates as a licensed tobacco retail and lounge venue in full compliance with Thai tobacco law. Smoking is permitted within our lounge premises.

Price ranges vary by brand and vitola -- from accessible entry-level cigars starting around 200 THB to rare and premium Cuban marques at 1,500 THB and above. Ask us directly for current pricing.

Yes. Amir offers B2B consulting for hotels, restaurants, and hospitality venues across Northern Thailand. This covers programme design, stock curation, humidification infrastructure, and staff training.

Walk-ins are welcome throughout opening hours -- no booking is required for individual visits. For private tastings, group visits, or events, we ask for advance notice. A quick WhatsApp message is the easiest way to let us know you are coming.
