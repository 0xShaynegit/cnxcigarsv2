## Use of Customer and Visitor Photography

CNX Cigars may photograph or record visitors to our lounge for the following purposes:

- Website content and marketing materials
- Social media promotion (Facebook, Instagram)
- Internal training and quality assurance
- Digital marketing and advertising
- Event documentation

## Consent

By visiting CNX Cigars and using our lounge, you consent to being photographed or recorded. If you do not wish to appear in photographs or videos, please notify a staff member immediately upon arrival, and we will ensure you are not included in any imagery captured during your visit.

## Photo Removal Request

If you have been photographed at CNX Cigars and would like your image removed from our website, social media, or other materials, please contact us immediately with:

- A description of the photograph or video
- Where you saw it published (website, Instagram, etc.)
- Your request for removal

We will make reasonable efforts to locate and remove the image within 14 days of your request.
