### Core Values

**Exceptional Provenance**
Every cigar in the humidor is selected by Amir Nadimi personally. Three decades spent tracking soil, climate, and craft mean you will never find a filler here. Only the genuine article, stored and presented exactly as it should be.

**Unmatched Expertise**
Amir speaks the language of draw, construction, and flavour evolution with the precision of a craftsman. Whether you are exploring your first cigar or chasing a rare vitola, the knowledge on offer here is real and freely shared.

**True Accessibility**
A private club with an open door. Amir has spent his career moving against the grain of gatekeeping. Seasoned aficionado or curious newcomer, you will find the same genuine welcome, the same honest guidance, and the same quality in the hand.

### Featured Cigars

**Romeo y Julieta Churchill**
- Strength: Medium
- Origin: Cuba
- Description: Elegant and aromatic, with cedar and a soft floral lift over gentle leather. A long, even, contemplative smoke.

**Cohiba Siglo VI**
- Strength: Medium
- Origin: Cuba
- Description: The pinnacle of the Linea Clasica. Honeyed, creamy and woody, layered and long. A special-occasion Habano.

**Padron 1926 Series No. 9**
- Strength: Full
- Origin: Nicaragua
- Description: Lavish and deep. Coffee, caramel and cocoa in a flawless box-pressed body. Among the most decorated cigars made.

**Oliva Serie V Melanio**
- Strength: Full
- Origin: Nicaragua
- Description: Full and refined at once. Espresso and cocoa with a structured black pepper spine and a long sweet finish.
